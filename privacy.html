Privacy

SuperMiniStream does not store or share any data about your use of it and is run locally through a Python script. Your YouTube account is used to create and manage the YouTube broadcasts and live streams for your conference. This is limited to: creating YouTube broadcasts and updating their descriptions with schedule information; creating YouTube live streams; binding and unbinding live streams to the YouTube broadcasts; and making the broadcasts live and complete. The data describing your conference sessions are provided locally by you and the application only interacts with the Youtube account, Zoom accounts and Discord Guild you provide it to schedule your conference's virtual components.

Limited Use Policy

SuperMiniStream's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

YouTube Terms of Service

SuperMiniStream is used to manage YouTube live streams, by using SuperMiniStream you are also agreeing to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service.

Google Privacy Policy

Use of SuperMiniStream is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Removing SuperMiniStream from Your Account

After your event ends, or if you no longer wish to use SuperMiniStream, you can revoke its access to your Google account via the Google security settings page.
